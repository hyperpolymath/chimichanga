## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

1. **Code Review**: Reviewing and merging pull requests
2. **Issue Triage**: Categorizing and responding to issues
3. **Security**: Responding to security reports (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md))
4. **Releases**: Managing version releases
5. **Community**: Fostering a healthy contributor community
